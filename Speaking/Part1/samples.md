# Speaking Part 1: Mẫu câu thông dụng

## Topic: Hobbies
- I’m really into [activity]...
- I’m a big fan of...
- Whenever I have spare time, I usually...
- It helps me to let my hair down / blow off some steam.

## Topic: Work/Study
- Currently, I am a student at [University Name] / I am working as a [Job] at [Company].
- What I like most about my job/school is the friendly environment.
- It is quite stressful/challenging but I learn a lot from it.

## Topic: Hometown
- I was born and raised in [City], which is located in the North/South of Vietnam.
- It is famous for [Food/Place].
- The people there are very hospitable and friendly.
