# Writing Task 1: Viết thư (Letter/Email)

## Thời gian: 20 phút (Nên viết khoảng 120 từ)

## Cấu trúc chuẩn (4 đoạn)

### 1. Salutation (Chào hỏi)
- Informal (Bạn bè): *Dear [Name], / Hi [Name],*
- Formal (Công việc/Than phiền): *Dear Sir/Madam,* hoặc *Dear Mr./Ms. [Last Name],*

### 2. Opening (Lý do viết thư)
- "I am writing this letter to..." (Formal)
- "How are you doing? I hope you are doing well. I'm writing to tell you about..." (Informal)
- Các mục đích thường gặp:
  - **Mời:** *invite you to...*
  - **Cảm ơn:** *thank you for...*
  - **Xin lỗi:** *apologize for...*
  - **Phàn nàn:** *complain about...*
  - **Yêu cầu:** *ask for information about...*

### 3. Body (Nội dung chính)
- Trả lời đầy đủ các yêu cầu (bullet points) trong đề bài.
- Sử dụng các từ nối: *First, Also, Besides...*

### 4. Closing (Kết thư)
- "I look forward to hearing from you soon."
- "Hope to see you soon."

### 5. Sign-off (Ký tên)
- Informal: *Best regards, / Love, / Cheers,*
- Formal: *Sincerely, / Yours faithfully,*

## Template mẫu (Email phàn nàn - Formal)
Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with the service/product I received at your store last [Time].

First of all, [Vấn đề 1 - Ví dụ: The food was cold]. This was very disappointing.
Secondly, [Vấn đề 2 - Ví dụ: The waiter was rude]. I expected better service from your restaurant.

Therefore, I would like to request a refund or a replacement. I hope you will resolve this issue promptly.

I look forward to your response.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
