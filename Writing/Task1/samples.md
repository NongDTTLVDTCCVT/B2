# Writing Task 1: Các mẫu câu thông dụng

## 1. Thư mời (Invitation)
- **Open:** "I'm having a birthday party next week and I would love to invite you to come."
- **Details:** "The party will be held at my house on Sunday evening."
- **Close:** "Let me know if you can make it."

## 2. Thư xin lỗi (Apology)
- **Open:** "I am terribly sorry for not attending your meeting yesterday."
- **Reason:** "Unfortunately, I had a sudden fever and had to go to the hospital."
- **Close:** "Please accept my sincere apologies. Can we reschedule?"

## 3. Thư yêu cầu thông tin (Request)
- **Open:** "I saw your advertisement for the English course and I am interested in joining."
- **Details:** "Could you please tell me about the tuition fee and the starting date?"
- **Close:** "Thank you in advance for your help."
