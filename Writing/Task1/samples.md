# Writing Task 1: Các mẫu câu thông dụng

## 1. Thư mời (Invitation)
### Useful Phrases
- **Open:** "I'm having a birthday party next week and I would love to invite you to come." (Informal) / "We would be honored to invite you to..." (Formal)
- **Details:** "The party will be held at my house on Sunday evening."
- **Close:** "Let me know if you can make it."

### Sample 1: Informal (Mời bạn sinh nhật)
Subject: Birthday Invite!

Hi Sarah,
Hope you're good.
I'm turning 21 next Saturday, and I'm having a small party at my place. There will be pizza, drinks, and karaoke! It wouldn’t be the same without you, so I really hope you can come.
Let me know if you can make it.
Best,
[Your Name]

### Sample 2: Formal (Mời dự hội nghị)
Subject: Invitation to the Annual Tech Conference

Dear Mr. Smith,
I am writing to cordially invite you to our Annual Tech Conference which will take place on November 15th at the City Convention Center.
The event will feature keynote speeches from industry leaders. We believe your expertise would be a great addition to our discussion panel.
Please confirm your attendance by November 1st. We look forward to welcoming you.
Sincerely,
[Your Name]

## 2. Thư xin lỗi (Apology)
### Useful Phrases
- **Open:** "I am terribly sorry for..." (Formal) / "I'm so sorry about..." (Informal)
- **Reason:** "Unfortunately, due to unforeseen circumstances..."
- **Close:** "Please accept my sincere apologies."

### Sample 1: Informal (Xin lỗi bạn vì lỡ hẹn)
Subject: So sorry!

Hi Tom,
I’m really sorry I couldn't make it to coffee yesterday. My bike broke down on the way, and my phone battery died so I couldn't text you.
I feel terrible about making you wait. Can I treat you to lunch next week to make up for it?
Let me know when you're free.
Cheers,
[Your Name]

### Sample 2: Formal (Xin lỗi khách hàng/sếp)
Subject: Apology for the late report

Dear Ms. Johnson,
Please accept my sincere apologies for sending the monthly report later than the deadline.
Unfortunately, I experienced some technical issues with my computer yesterday, which resulted in the loss of some data. I have now recovered the files and completed the report attached below.
I will ensure this does not happen again. Thank you for your understanding.
Regards,
[Your Name]

## 3. Thư yêu cầu thông tin (Request)
### Useful Phrases
- **Open:** "I saw your advertisement and I am interested in..." (Formal) / "Can you tell me..." (Informal)
- **Details:** "Could you please provide more details regarding..."
- **Close:** "I look forward to hearing from you."

### Sample 1: Informal (Hỏi bạn thông tin du lịch)
Subject: Question about Da Nang trip

Hey Anna,
How’s it going?
I saw your photos from Da Nang on Facebook – they looked amazing! I’m planning to go there next month with my family.
Could you recommend some good but affordable hotels near the beach? Also, do we need to book tours in advance?
Thanks a lot via help!
Talk soon,
[Your Name]

### Sample 2: Formal (Hỏi thông tin khóa học)
Subject: Inquiry regarding IELTS Preparation Course

Dear Sir/Madam,
I am writing to ask for some information about the IELTS preparation course advertised on your website.
I am planning to take the exam in August and currently aim for a band 6.5. Could you please let me know the course schedule, tuition fee, and the maximum number of students per class?
I would appreciate it if you could send me the syllabus as well.
Thank you in advance.
Yours faithfully,
[Your Name]
